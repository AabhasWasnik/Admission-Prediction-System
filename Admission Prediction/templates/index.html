Vision
Our vision for the graduate admission prediction system is to revolutionize the admissions process by providing accurate and fair predictions of an applicant's likelihood of being admitted to a post graduate program. We aim to help graduate students make informed decisions based on data-driven insights and reduce biases in the admissions process.
Mission
Our mission is to develop and implement a graduate admission prediction system that leverages machine learning algorithms and historical data to provide accurate predictions of an applicant's likelihood of being admitted to a graduate program. We aim to provide transparency and fairness in the admissions process by considering a range of factors, including academic and non-academic factors, and by reducing the influence of biases. Our system will enable graduate programs to make more informed decisions, save time, and increase the diversity and quality of their applicant pool.
